KMCT INSITITUTE OF TECHONOLOGY AND MANAGMENT

Established in 2014 by Dr. Navas KM, KMCT Arts and Science College, Kuttippuram, is affiliated with the University of Calicut, approved by the Government of Kerala, and recognized by the UGC under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act 1956. KMCT Arts and Science College is a minority status institution with a mission of serving humanity by inculcating supreme moral values in its students. The college is run by the Kunhitharuvai Memorial Charitable Trust (KMCT), with a key determination to provide quality education for socially and economically backward classes in this area. A vast number of students from different parts of the district are molded in this institution.
