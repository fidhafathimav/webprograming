MCA stands for Master of Computer Applications. It is a 2-year postgraduate degree program designed for students who want to specialize in computer applications and software development.To be eligible for an MCA program, you typically need to have a bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, or a related field with a minimum of 50-60% aggregate marks. Some universities may also require candidates to appear for entrance exams Admission to MCA programs is often based on merit or entrance exams. Some common entrance exams for MCA include CAT (Common Admission Test), MAT, XAT and IIFT.
